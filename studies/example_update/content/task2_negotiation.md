# Negotiation Task

## Overview

In this task you will participate in a business negotiation simulation. You will negotiate with another party (simulated) to reach an agreement.

## Instructions

1. **Prepare**: Before entering the negotiation, think about your strategy. What are your goals? What are you willing to concede? What is your best alternative if no deal is reached?
2. **Negotiate**: Use the link below to open the negotiation app. Follow the instructions in the app to conduct your negotiation.
3. **Reflect**: After the negotiation, describe your strategy and outcome in the text field below.

If an AI assistant is available, you may use it to help prepare your strategy, analyse the scenario, and develop your arguments.
