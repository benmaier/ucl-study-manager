# Data Analysis Task

## Scenario

You are assisting a professor in evaluating the outcome of an anti-discrimination campaign across schools in the US conducted for one year in 2000.

You have access to the professor's data folder to complete the analysis. Unfortunately, the professor let their kid play with the folder, so it may contain unnecessary files, and some data files may be corrupted or unreliable.

## Your Task

Download and examine the available data files. Analyse the data to answer the questions below. You are responsible for verifying the quality and relevance of each data source before drawing conclusions.

You may use any tools available to you (AI assistant if provided, spreadsheet software, Python, etc.) but you may not discuss with anyone else.

**Note:** There are trick questions. Think carefully about what the data actually shows.
